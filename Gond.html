Gond Art

Gond art is a vibrant tribal art form practiced by the Gond tribe of central India, particularly in Madhya Pradesh. It is characterized by its intricate patterns, bright colors, and imaginative depiction of flora, fauna, and deities. Traditionally, Gond paintings were created on mud walls using natural colors, but today they are also made on paper and canvas. The art form often reflects folk tales, nature, and spiritual themes with a distinctive use of dots, dashes, and lines to create texture and rhythm.
